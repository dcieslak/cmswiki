Menu & sidebars

In order to alter menu & sidebars find them using "search" with empty search string:
  • [[menu]]
  • [[sidebar]]
  • [[rightbar]]
Click on them and click "edit" - you can alter menu & sidebars then. Extremely simple IMHO ;-)
