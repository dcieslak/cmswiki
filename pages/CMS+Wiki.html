[[CMS Wiki]] is dumb and simple Content Management System for your webpage that connects WYSIWYG (What You See Is What You Get) editing with Wiki page creation and linking.

Main [[CMS Wiki]] benefits:
  • Runs on every PHP-able account
  • Doesn't require any database (just flat HTML files)
  • Very simple to install (see: [[Installation]] (unpack & go!)
  • Very simple to use (see: [[Usage]]), rules:
    • just click edit
    • link pages by surrounding new page title with [[...]],
    • no more rules!
  • Simple to change template, see: [[Template Change]]
  • Secure: edit script can (should?) be protected by Apache standard and well tested mechanism (htaccess / htpasswd)
  • Very fast: almost pure php page! Compare it with very thick TWiki and see the difference!
