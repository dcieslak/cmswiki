1. Grab package from [[Download]] page link
  2. Upload to your WWW account
  3. Ensure your pages subdirectory is writable by Apache user
  4. Direct your browser to http://yourserver/install_dir
  5. Enjoy!
Optional, but very important steps:
  1. Protect your edit.php script by .htaccess (Apache)
Options -Indexes                                                                                                                                             
AuthType Basic                                                                                                                                               
AuthName "Aplikacja.info - CMSWIKI"                                                                                                                          
AuthUserFile "/home/your-path-to-htpasswd-dir/.htpasswd"                                                                                                               
DirectoryIndex view.php                                                                                                                                      
                                                                                                                                                             
<Files "edit.php">                                                                                                                                           
    require valid-user
</Files>
